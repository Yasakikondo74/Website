Shinhan Sol 3.0

March - August 2022

About the Project

SOL Mobile Banking is Shinhan Bank's mobile banking application, supporting customer to conduct financial transactions safety, quickly and conveniently right on their mobile phone. In this project, we aim to revamp the app - giving it a whole new look along with UX enhancements, looking to solve the problems the current SOL 2.0 are reported to be having.

Scope of work

  • Carry out UXUI audit, stakeholders’ interviews, and user research as part of the discovery phrase
  • Ideate, brainstorm and construct new sitemap to enhance navigation within SOL
  • Put the ideas we brainstorm together with customers into wireframes and prototype
  • Design UI screens based on Mother bank’s design system integrated with localization
  • Carry out user testings for lo-fi prototype and UI demo screens
  • Establish design guideline with stylings and components from Mother bank for further development
