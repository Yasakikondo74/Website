About the Project

SOL Mobile Banking is Shinhan Bank's mobile banking application, supporting customer to conduct financial transactions safety, quickly and conveniently right on their mobile phone. In this project, we aim to revamp the app - giving it a whole new look along with UX enhancements, looking to solve the problems the current SOL 2.0 are reported to be having.
